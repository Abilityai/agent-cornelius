5. Right Livelihood - Living without harm

## üí° Key Buddhist Insights

### 1. Reality Doesn't Need Explanation
- [[In Buddhism each attempt to Explain Reality removes the ability to directly experience it]]
- Truth needs only to be seen, not defined
- Language describes in limited concepts
- Direct perception > conceptual understanding

### 2. Suffering Comes from Within
- [[In Buddhism - desire to achieve or get rid of something creates Duhkha]]
- Not external circumstances, but internal relationship to them
- Craving (to get) and aversion (to get rid of) create suffering

### 3. Self is Not What We Think
- [[In Buddhism - Self is an Illusion]]
- [[Conceptualization of the experience creates Illusion of Self]]
- No fixed, separate, enduring self can be found
- Experience exists, but no experiencer to locate

### 4. Freedom Through Seeing
- [[Awakening and Enlightenment are about being in the Moment, in Here and Now]]
- Not adding concepts, but removing conceptual overlay
- Present-moment awareness without leaning

### 5. You Are Your Own Authority
- No external teaching can give you enlightenment
- Must test everything yourself
- Awakening = seeing for yourself, not believing others
