## üéØ Practical Applications

### Optimization Strategies
- Remove layering (multiple dopamine sources simultaneously)
- Intermittent reinforcement (don't celebrate every win)
- Learn to enjoy effort, not just outcomes ([[PFC]] control)
- Cold water exposure for baseline increase

### Warnings
- [[If you like something, and then start giving yourself a reward for this, and then take away the reward - you will stop liking the activity]]
- Don't spike dopamine before or after effort - engage it AT the effort
- Tolerance develops: More needed for same effect
