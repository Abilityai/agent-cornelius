## üéØ Key Insights

### Why Identity-Linked Beliefs Resist Change
1. **Neurological cost:** Dopamine withdrawal from losing certainty
2. **Social cost:** Risk of group rejection
3. **Cognitive cost:** Rebuilding worldview framework
4. **Reward loss:** Giving up confirmation dopamine hits

### The Modern Identity Crisis
- Social media accelerates identity formation
- Individual identity merges with group identity
- Algorithmic exploitation of identity conflict
- Polarization as emergent property of platform design

### Buddhist-Neuroscience Consilience
Both domains recognize:
- Self/identity as constructed, not fundamental
- Attachment to identity creates suffering/rigidity
- Liberation requires releasing identification with beliefs
- Direct experience vs. conceptual overlay
