# LLM God Mind as Collective Unknown

Intelligence can be defined as a different topology - the collective unknown of all different LLMs. This "god mind" is the theoretical complete knowledge graph if all LLMs were merged, but it's unreachable by any single entity.

The critical question: Is this "god mind" the average (consensus across models) or the union (complete possibility space)?

- **Average = narrow but reliable** - Where models agree, high confidence
- **Union = vast but contradictory** - All possible responses, mutually exclusive

This reframes AI intelligence from individual systems to an emergent distributed property. No single model contains "the truth" - truth becomes a property of the collective system, inaccessible to individual actors (including humans).

**Key implication:** We're not building intelligence, we're discovering different slices of a larger intelligence topology that no single entity can fully access.
