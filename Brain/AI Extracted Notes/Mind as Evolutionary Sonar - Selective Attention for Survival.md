**Core Insight** (in author's voice):

Our mind is a sort of a sonar that scans for threats and opportunities. This is its main evolutionary objective. So it can focus on a narrow spectrum of things at one time, it cannot take a broader perspective and see everything at the same time. For your animal mind it is important to quickly identify say a lion in the bush, otherwise you don't survive. So brain has a function of selecting objects from the surrounding and putting them in a separate bucket (say threats, lions).

---

**Context & Reasoning**:

**The Sonar Metaphor - Why It's Powerful:**
- Sonar scans sequentially, not comprehensively (narrow beam)
- Prioritizes survival-relevant signals (threats, opportunities)
- Active scanning (sending out signals, waiting for returns)
- Creates discrete objects from continuous environment
- **Evolution optimized for speed (quick lion identification) over comprehension (full environmental understanding)**

**Evolutionary Mechanism:**
- Survival required FAST categorization, not complete perception
- "Lion in bush" detection > philosophical contemplation of oneness
- Selective attention = survival advantage
- Comprehensive awareness = evolutionary disadvantage (slow, vulnerable)

**This Gives Birth to Conceptualization:**
- Sonar identifies discrete objects → brain creates categories
- Categories enable fast pattern-matching (is this a lion?)
- Speed requirement → simplified mental buckets (threat/not-threat)
- **Survival pressure drove conceptual splitting of unified reality**

**Bridge to Buddhism:**
- Buddhism: Conceptualization splits reality, creates duality
- This insight: Explains WHY evolution built minds that conceptualize
- The "illusion" of separate objects served survival function
- Modern problem: Survival mechanism now creates suffering (Duhkha)

**Why Consciousness Can't See Everything at Once:**
- Evolutionary trade-off: Speed vs. Comprehension
- Sonar beam width = attention bandwidth
- Processing capacity allocated to survival-critical objects
- Remaining environment = background noise (filtered out)
