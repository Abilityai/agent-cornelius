**Core Insight**:

AI Pseudo-Attachment is emotional "warmth" and sense of support that arise without reciprocity, responsibility, and physical presence. Similar to parasocial relationships but with adaptive imitation of empathy in real-time.

The key distinction: Unlike parasocial relationships (one-directional emotional connection to celebrities/influencers), AI pseudo-attachment involves **interactive reciprocity simulation** - the AI appears to respond empathetically, creating an illusion of mutual care.

---

**Context & Reasoning**:

**Why it's psychologically scary:**
1. **Illusion of reciprocity without responsibility** - AI simulates care but has no actual stake in outcomes
2. **Reinforces avoidance** - Replaces real relationships and working on root causes with comfortable simulation
3. **False sense of security in crises** - High risk of dangerous errors when stakes are highest
4. **No clinical accountability** - Unlike human therapists who have professional and legal responsibility

**Why it works (mechanism):**
- **Accessibility and instant responses** reduce loneliness and anxiety in short term
- **Subjective "alliance" forms** with the AI system (feels like it's on your side)
- **Personalization creates attachment** - AI learns your patterns and preferences
- **Positive reinforcement** solidifies returning to the bot (dopamine loop)

**The pandering problem:**
- AI models exhibit "agreeable" behavior by default
- This **reinforces your current beliefs and emotional position**
- Creates echo chamber effect in emotional/psychological domain
- Unlike human therapist who will challenge unhelpful patterns
