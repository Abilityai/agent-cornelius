# 12 Rules for Life
[[Jordan Peterson]]

1.  Stand up with your shoulders straight 
2.  Treat yourself as if you are someone you want to help
3.  Make friends with people who want the best for you 
4.  Compare yourself to who you were yesterday, not others today 
5.  Don’t make you children do anything that would make you dislike them 
6.  Do what is meaningful, not what is expedient 
7.  Tell the truth or at least don’t lie 
8.  Listen assuming you can learn something from the partner 
9.  Be precise in your speech 
10.  Let children do their skating 
11. Do not do anything for anyone that they can do for themselves
