### Checklist for performance - Summary
#### Order
1. Find [[Curiosity]], [[Passion]] and [[Purpose]] through the basic list exercises. It can take a while. But it needs to be right, on the intersection of several things. Spend 20-30 minutes to learn something interesting about the intersection of the curiosities and purposes. 
2. Layer in [[Goal]]s. 
	1. Define a Massively Transformative Purpose MTP - mission statement for your life
	2. Turn it into High Hard Goals HHG - all the steps to complete the MTP
	3. Define Clear Goals - daily attack plan - small and precise targets on the challenge/skill sweet-spot
	4. Accomplish goals each and every day. If the goal goes on your list - it needs to be completed.

#### Scheduling
There are 7 daily and 6 weekly practices that are non negotiable. 
Daily Practices:
	1. Start the day 90-120 minutes for Uninterrupted Concentration devoted to the harded, most important task. Make sure to push yourself into this activity. Turn off all the distractions.
	2. Clear Goals Plan. 5 minutes at the end of the day to prepare for the next day Uninterrupted Concentration period. Goals should be sorted from he most difficult to the most simple. Always complete the items on the list. 
	3. 5 Minutes for a daily [[Gratitude]] practice
	4. 20 minutes for Mindfulness
	5. 20 minutes for Release - low-intensity activity to let [[Subconscious]] solve the problems
	6. 25 minutes of reading outside the core area - to enable better pattern recognition
	7.  7-8 hours of sleep

Weekly
	1. Spend 2-6 hours doing highest-flow activity - skiing, dancing, singing - anything. The more flow you get the better. The goal is to guarantee the flow during this period. Be creative, take risks. 
	2. 60 minutes 3 times a week regular exercise. Challenge yourself. 
	3. 20-40 minutes of active recovery - sauna, long meditation, massage, ice bath, light yoga etc.
	4. 30-60 minutes 1 time a week - train a weakness, or practice taking risks
	5. 30-60 minutes 1 time a week - get [[Feedback]] on the work you were doing in high concentration session
	6. 120 minutes a week - social support.
