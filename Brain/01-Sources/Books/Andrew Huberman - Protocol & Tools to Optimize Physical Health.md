Week Protocol:
 1 day - one aspect of fitness. 

180 minutes of Zone 2 Cardio is a must. 
Get to max cardio at least once a week

Alternate between lower reps and higher weight, and more reps with lower weight - month to month. 

**Day 1: Sunday**
Endurance Day
60-75 minutes of jogging, Roving, cycling, swimming, or 3 hours of hiking
Possibly with a weight vest. 
Being able to sustain a conversation. 

**Day 2: Monday**
Morning Leg Workout - Cavs, Quadriceps, 
About 10 minutes of warmup
50-60 of hard work 
2 exercises by muscle group - 1 for contracting and 1 for stretching the same muscle group. 
3 / 12

**Day 3: Tuesday**
Stretching
Heat-Cold Contrasts 
Sauna + Ice Bath
1 day a week and a lot of sessions a time

**Day 4: Wednesday**
Training a torso. All muscle groups at the same day. 
Push-Pull Exercises mixed together
2 per muscle group. 

**Day 5: Thursday**
Cardio - about 45 minutes of running. 
Warmup. 
75-80% of all-out
More than Zone 2. 

**Day 6: Friday** 
HIIT - High Intensity Interval Training
20 sec sprint + 10 sec rest (8-12 rounds)
Get heart rate really high once a week

**Day 7: Saturday**
Arms, Cavs, Neck, Dip Movement
