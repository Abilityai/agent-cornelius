[[Market Price in terms of other liquid assets is more important than monetary]]
[[Investing in pairs makes more sense- shorting one asset and longing another]]

So, the strategy during the crisis time should be to buy stocks where demand depends less on consumers and more on required goods, like energy, food.

It also makes sense to take those who can pay out dividends.

The only exception are the companies who have significant amount of their own stock or related index funds - in this case they will be affected by the drop of the market.

Interestingly, the goal of the board of directors (fedutiary duties) is to increase the shareholder value, rather than keep the sales up. Which motivates them to e.g. buy stocks of their own company instead of paying.
