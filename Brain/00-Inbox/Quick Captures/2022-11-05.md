Technology [[Innovation]] moves much faster than people's ability to take it in, to learn it. Now we are in a situation that knowing about the most advanced [[Technology]] like the most recent achievements in [[AI]] and [[Blockchain]] create a [[Competitive Advantage]]. 

This also means that in order to build a successful a company, you don't need to use the latest [[Technology]] - you just need to successfully sell something people are ready to buy, ready to understand. 

Many people would rather buy something good enough that they understand, than something amazing that seems like magic to them. Because it create [[Fear]] and scares them off. Because they can't Explain it. [[Explanation]]

Interesting #insight from the book [[Stumbling on Happiness - Daniel Gilbert]]. One of the conclusions there is that when you imagine how something will feel you imagine how you would feel now and then adjust for time. May be, in order to be consistent with ourselves, we need to try to make those [[prediction]]s match the result, thus we are not allowing ourselves to change what we feel with time. Keep our [[Identity]] stable.
