## Core Insight

Production-deployed AI agents across multiple enterprises now achieve **80%+ query resolution rates** without human intervention (up from 45% in 2023), with response times under 2 seconds and error rates below 5% in standard operations. Market data shows average **$2.3M annual cost reduction** per deployed agent with **43% efficiency gains**.

## Context & Evidence

**Klarna (Early 2024)**:
- Handled ~67% of incoming support chats in first month
- **2.3 million conversations** managed monthly
- Resolution time: ~11 minutes → **under 2 minutes**
- Capacity equivalent: ~700 FTE

**Intercom/Synthesia (2024, 6 months)**:
- 1,300+ support hours saved
- 6,000+ conversations resolved
- During **690% volume spike**: 98.3% of users self-served without human escalation

**Esusu (Email automation)**:
- 64% of email interactions automated
- **10-point CSAT lift** (customer satisfaction increased)
- First reply time: Down 64%
- Resolution time: Down 34%

**Stream (Internal operations)**:
- 80%+ of internal customer inquiries handled (pay dates, balances, etc.)
- Gemini models for employee-facing queries

**Industry Averages (2025)**:
- 80% query resolution without human intervention (vs. 45% in 2023)
- Error rates below 5% in standard operations
- Response times averaging under 2 seconds
