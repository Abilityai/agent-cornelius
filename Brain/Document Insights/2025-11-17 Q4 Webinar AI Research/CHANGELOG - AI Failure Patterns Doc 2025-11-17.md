## Unique Insights Created

### Strategic Failure Patterns

1. **[[The Vision Vacuum creates the Use Case Trap - strategic failure cascade]]**
   - C-suite vision absence → bottom-up tactical PoC proliferation → $5M-$20M waste per abandoned PoC
   - 30% PoC abandonment + 42% strategic abandonment (2.5x increase from 2024)
   - Forrester: "Vision Vacuum" fills with "Use Case Trap" → illusion of progress

2. **[[CEO-level governance most correlated with AI success]]**
   - McKinsey March 2025: CEO oversight = highest correlation with P&L impact
   - Not micromanagement; strategic priority signaling and cross-functional authority
   - 6% High Performers have CEO governance; 95% don't

3. **[[The Great AI Divide - 95 percent zero return vs 6 percent transformative impact]]**
   - MIT: 95% get zero P&L return despite $30-40B investment
   - McKinsey: 6% achieve 5%+ EBIT impact
   - Bimodal distribution: organizational maturity gap, not technology gap
   - Reconciles MIT 95% failure vs Google 74% success (tactical vs transformative)

4. **[[The 2.5x abandonment surge despite better AI - strategic failure accelerating]]**
   - 2024: 17% scrapping most initiatives → 2025: 42% scrapping most initiatives
   - Technology improved (GPT-4 → GPT-4o), failures accelerated
   - Proves strategic failure, not technical; market shifting from "doesn't work" to "not worth it"

### Operational Failure Patterns

5. **[[The AI Productivity Paradox - individual speed up organizational throughput flat]]**
   - Bain/Faros: +98% PRs, +21% tasks completed, but zero company throughput improvement
   - Root cause: Optimized 25-35% of workflow (coding), created bottleneck in 65-75% (review)
   - +91% review time increase (productivity gains pile up at bottleneck)
   - "AI-Assisted" (insert into old process) vs "AI-Centric" (redesign process)

6. **[[The Verification Tax - hidden cost that evaporates AI productivity gains]]**
   - AI "confidently wrong" → humans must double-check everything
   - Verification work is harder than original (must spot subtle errors in plausible outputs)
   - Promised productivity gains "often evaporate" under verification load
   - Not just internal inefficiency; existential reputational risk (Deloitte case)

7. **[[Workflow redesign non-negotiable - distinguishes 6 percent from 95 percent]]**
   - McKinsey: 6% "fundamentally redesign workflows" (AI-Centric)
   - 95% "insert AI into old processes" (AI-Assisted)
   - THE operational differentiator between success and failure
   - Must redesign entire system, not just optimize one step

8. **[[Pilot Purgatory - $5M-$20M per POC creates massive sunk cost trap]]**
   - GenAI PoC costs: $5M-$20M each (Gartner)
   - 30-42% abandonment rate → tens of millions wasted per enterprise
   - Organizations stuck: too expensive to abandon, too unproven to scale
   - Sunk cost psychology meets strategic failure

### Organizational Dynamics

9. **[[Trust as 4x organizational multiplier - 57 percent vs 14 percent differential]]**
   - High-maturity: 57% business unit trust
   - Low-maturity: 14% business unit trust (4x differential)
   - "Adoption is the first step in generating value" (Gartner)
   - Trust drives adoption; adoption drives value (flywheel)

10. **[[Shadow AI outperforms formal projects - the bottom-up paradox]]**
    - MIT: Shadow AI (personal ChatGPT, $20/month) delivers better ROI than $5M-$20M formal projects
    - Bottom-up, grassroots > top-down, strategic
    - Reveals organizational strategy is broken (not technology)
    - Individuals have clear use case; organizations stuck in Vision Vacuum

### Root Cause Reframing

11. **[[Data problem is symptom not cause - strategic failure disguised as technical]]**
    - 60% abandoned for "lack of AI-ready data" (Gartner)
    - But "AI-ready" = "fit for purpose" → requires defining PURPOSE
    - Can't define purpose in Vision Vacuum
    - Data problem is third domino, not first (Strategic → Operational → Foundational)
    - Easier to blame technical issue (data) than admit strategic failure (no vision)

### Case Studies (2025 Landmark Failures)

12. **[[Deloitte AI failure - control failure not technology failure]]**
    - A$440K government report with fabricated citations, fake court quotes
    - Classified as "leadership failure," "control failure"—NOT tech failure
    - Over-reliance on AI without verification process
    - Reputational damage >> contract value (trust is primary asset for professional services)

13. **[[The Klarna Reversal - overestimating AI led to rehiring after mass layoffs]]**
    - Cut 25% workforce in 2024 "anticipating AI-led future"
    - Forced to rehire in 2025; "human touch remains irreplaceable"
    - Failed to redesign workflow; tried 1:1 human → AI replacement
    - Jumped from Old Process → AI-Only (skipped hybrid AI-Assisted phase)
