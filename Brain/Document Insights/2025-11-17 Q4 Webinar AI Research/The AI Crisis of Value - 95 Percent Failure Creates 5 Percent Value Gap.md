## Core Insight

The 2025 AI market is defined by a "crisis of value" where 95% of implementations fail to deliver measurable P&L impact despite $30-40 billion in enterprise investment, while only 5% of firms - the "AI-future built" - achieve five times the revenue increases and three times the cost reductions. This extreme bifurcation creates a "Widening AI Value Gap" where the choice of implementation strategy (Build vs. Buy vs. Partner) is the key determinant of which group a company falls into.

---

## Context & Evidence

**The Failure Statistics:**
- **95% Failure Rate**: Most AI pilots remain "stuck with no measurable P&L impact" despite investment (MIT 2025)
- **60% Reaping "Hardly Any Value"**: Despite AI investment, majority of firms see no material returns (BCG 2025)
- **5% Success Rate**: Only 5% are "AI-future built" and successfully capturing value (BCG 2025)
- **The Performance Gap**: The successful 5% achieve 5x the revenue increases and 3x the cost reductions compared to others

**Why the 95% Fail:**
1. **Endless Experimentation**: Stuck in pilot phase, unable to scale to production
2. **Misaligned Resources**: Building commodity functions instead of strategic differentiators
3. **Lack of Top-Down Agenda**: No multiyear strategic plan for AI transformation
4. **Missing Critical Capabilities**: Lack of governance, architecture, or partnership strategy
5. **Wrong Implementation Path**: Pure in-house builds without expertise (75% failure rate)

**Why the 5% Succeed:**
1. **Strategic Focus**: Build the 10-15% that differentiates, buy the rest
2. **Top-Down Multiyear Agenda**: Clear strategic plan with C-suite alignment
3. **Reshape, Don't Just Automate**: Use AI to "reshape and invent" core workflows, not just automate
4. **Critical Internal Capabilities**: Strong governance, architecture, and partnership management
5. **De-Risked Execution**: Partner-led builds for strategic projects (2x success rate)

**The Market Pivot:**
The entire market is pivoting from "endless experimentation" (2024) to C-suite-driven demand for "scaled, production-grade AI" and measurable P&L impact (2025). The crisis of value is forcing companies to formalize their implementation strategy or remain stuck in the 95%.
