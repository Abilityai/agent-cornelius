## Core Insight

In 2024, Generative AI was treated as a separate, magical category. By 2025, that distinction has collapsed - positive ROI from GenAI is now on par with traditional predictive AI. Customers no longer "buy GenAI"; they buy solutions (sales automation, knowledge systems) and expect GenAI as an integrated feature enhancing core value.

- Don't lead with "GenAI-powered" - that's now table stakes
- Lead with business outcome and workflow transformation

**Strategic Context:**
This convergence accelerates the flight to safety. Organizations care about:
1. Business value delivered
2. Predictable implementation
3. Measurable ROI
4. Workflow integration

They don't care about whether it's:
1. GenAI vs Predictive AI
2. Transformer vs GAN
3. GPT-4 vs Claude

**Market Maturation Signal:**
When customers stop asking about the technology and start demanding proof of business value, the market has matured. 2025 is that inflection point for GenAI.
