## Core Insight

The field of multi-agent AI has undergone a paradigm shift from studying whether agents can coordinate (descriptive science) to understanding how to systematically engineer emergent collective intelligence (prescriptive engineering). This maturation is evidenced by rigorous measurement frameworks (information theory), practical deployment tools (AutoGen, CrewAI, Swarm), theoretical foundations (coordination dynamics, consensus theory), and cross-domain synthesis.
