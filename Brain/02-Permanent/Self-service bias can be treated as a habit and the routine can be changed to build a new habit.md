Self-service bias is when you quickly ascribe all the achievements to your skill and others to their luck. This is a harmful thing that does not allow you to update your beliefs. 

[[Black and white thinking causes both Self-service bias and Motivated reasoning]]

In some sense, it can be looked at as a [[Habit]], where someone's success is a [[Cue]] and your judgement about it is a routine, which followed by a reward to feeling goof about yourself. Creating a story about yourself. 

If we treat it as a Habit, then 

[[To change the habit we need to change the routine but keep the cue and reward]]
[[Mindfulness makes it possible to change habits for the better]]
