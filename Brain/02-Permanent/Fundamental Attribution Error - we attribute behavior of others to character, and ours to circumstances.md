When we think about other people's actions, we attribute them to who they are, to intrinsic characteristics of them, part of their [[Self]]. 

When it comes to us, we think that our behavior is more driven by a situation and circumstances in this situation. 

This can result in social conflicts, that are usually around [[Identity]] and personality.
