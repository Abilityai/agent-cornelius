Since [[In Noisy systems errors don't cancel out they add up]]
one way to avoid it is to have several estimates and average them out. Then errors and Noise in each of the estimates will cancel out. 
This can be applied to both [[Forecast]] and [[Estimate]]. 

This is basically how [[Wisdom of a crowd]] works.
