**Bets, [[Skin In The Game]] forces us to review our believes and avoid [[Confirmation bias]].** 

So the more you put [[Skin In The Game]] the more you are forced to update your believes. And the more correct the believes are - the better decisions you make overall. So the more [[Skin In The Game]] you have the more Chances you have to be successful.
