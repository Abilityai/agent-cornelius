Details is what differentiates objects close and far ahead. Our brain understands that something is close based on how detailed the picture of this object. The same thing happens with the events we imagine in the future. The closer the event in time, the more details we can imagine. 

[[Suffering in the near future is perceived as harder than suffering in the far future]]
