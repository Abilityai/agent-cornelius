[[Attribute Substitution]] or Bait&Switch - when people are replacing the hard question with an easy one to be able to answer it. The simpler question becomes a proxy for the more complex one. And [[Availability Bias]] is basically this same approach - we grab whatever simple is available as an answer to avoid the more complex one.
