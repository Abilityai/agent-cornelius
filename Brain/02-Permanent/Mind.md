# Mind

- Split Brain research suggests that two minds are available, they are independent and may have their own Self in each of them. One of them can talk (left) and another can not, but still can answer and make judgements.
- Theory of Mind might be directly connected to the feeling of [[Self]]  through the mechanism of imagining what would that feel like if someone was looking, what would my Self do, how would it feel.
- It might be that the feeling of Self is simply an illusion built up on the society perception of us
