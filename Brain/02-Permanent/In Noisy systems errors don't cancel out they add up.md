[[Noise]] [[Decision Making]] [[Noise A Flaw in Human Judgment - Kahneman]] [[Judgment]]
One can assume that Noise will cancel out if judgments are making errors into both sides. Positive and negative. It would be the case if these were judgments of the same case, and the result would the the average of those judgments. But in reality most of the cases are singular and thus positive and negative errors add up.
