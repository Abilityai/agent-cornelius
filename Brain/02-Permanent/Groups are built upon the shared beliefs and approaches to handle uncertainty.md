[[Confirmatory Groups promote biases]]

[[Confirmatory Group]]s and other social groups are built on the basis of having shared beliefs, which is a shared way to deal with [[Uncertainty]]. It gives a comfort to the group members that their beliefs about the world are confirmed by other group members, and they are more likely to be true.
