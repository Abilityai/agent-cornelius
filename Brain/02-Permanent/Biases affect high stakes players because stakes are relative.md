It would seem that the higher the stakes are the more rational agents become, but this is not the case. 

The reason biases and cognitive errors do not disappear when the stakes are high is because the size of the stakes is relative. For someone with net worth of 10k, dealing with 5K decision is huge, but for someone with higher net worth this is nothing.
