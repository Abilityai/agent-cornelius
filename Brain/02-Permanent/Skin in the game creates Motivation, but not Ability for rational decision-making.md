On the one hand, Skin in the game is necessary to want to update the beliefs and to not fall victim to Confirmation Bias, so it creates the [[Motivation]] to be rational. 

But it does not create the ability to be rational. So for those people who don't operate with rationality, don't know how to operate with it - Skin in the game will be a detrimental factor.
