[[Direct Comparison makes us pay attention to previously unimportant facts]]

[[Value]] is determined by the [[comparison]] of one thing to another. When we compare things, we use multitude of metrics and factors to compare. But to make the right choice about the future, we need to make a choice based on the factors that we will worry about in the future, and not the ones we worry about now.
