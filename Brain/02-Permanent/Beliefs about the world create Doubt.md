[[Doubt]] is a flip-side of [[Belief]]. As soon as we have a belief - doubt arises. The moment we have a solid idea about Reality (belief) - we inevitably give rise to anxiety and fear. Instead we should only see, perceive Reality as it is. 

[[Belief is a way to deal with Uncertainty, to explain parts of the world that we can't explain]]
