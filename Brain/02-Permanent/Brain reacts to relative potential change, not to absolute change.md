People react to the changes and differences - to the relative value of something, rather than the absolute one. E.g. percentage changes of the weight of the object, rather than absolute one. [[Potential Change]]
People are ready to spend hours saving $50 of the $100 item, then $50 from the $10K item, even though this is the same $50.
