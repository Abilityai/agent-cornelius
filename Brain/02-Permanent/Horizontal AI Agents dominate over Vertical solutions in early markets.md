# Horizontal AI Agents dominate over Vertical solutions in early markets

In the 2024 AI agents market, horizontal solutions (working across industries) are far more common than vertical agent solutions. This suggests early-stage markets favor generalist approaches over industry-specific specialization.

**Source:** [[AI Agents in August 2024 - State of the Union]] (YourWay Substack)
**Date:** 2024-08-21
**Type:** Market Pattern Recognition

## Connections

- [[Platform economics]] - Horizontal platforms achieve network effects faster
- [[Survivorship Bias]] - We may only see surviving horizontal players, not failed vertical attempts
- Market dynamics favor breadth over depth in early adoption phases

## Strategic Implications

- Vertical specialization may come later in market maturity
- Horizontal solutions easier to fund/build in uncertain markets
- Industry-specific knowledge compounds difficulty of agent development
- Generalist agents face commoditization risk once market matures

## Questions

- Do vertical agents require more domain expertise that's scarce?
