## Why Smart People Fall for This Trap

### Factor 1: Previous Success Pattern

- Traditional IT pattern worked for decades
- [[Dopamine spikes reinforce previous actions and create Learning]]
- Hard to recognize when learned pattern becomes harmful
- [[With repeated exposure to same stimulus Dopamine spikes weaken]]
- But pattern persists even when dopamine fades

### Factor 2: Identity Protection

- [[Identity is a set of beliefs about the world]]
- "We're thorough professionals who don't cut corners"
- Deploying with imperfect data threatens this identity
- [[People strive to confirm beliefs that are part of their identity]]
- Data cleaning confirms thoroughness identity

### Factor 3: Uncertainty Avoidance

- [[Human brain looks for certainty and doesn't like uncertainty]]
- AI deployment = massive uncertainty
- Data cleaning = concrete, knowable problem
- Trading effective but uncertain action for ineffective but certain action

### Factor 4: Social Validation

- [[Social Groups help find confirmations to reinforce the beliefs]]
- Gartner, analysts, consultants all emphasize "data strategy"
- Creates social proof for preparation-first approach
- [[Groups are built upon the shared beliefs and approaches to handle uncertainty]]
- IT community shares "clean data first" belief
