[[Debt The First 5000 Years - David Graeber]]

According to the author, the first [[economy]] engine was a [[Credit]] - when the [[Debt]] is calculated based on an assumed value of known goods, like sheep, or sacks of wheat. So all the relationships were built on trust and credit. Only then came [[money]] as an intermediary of exchange, and only then barter.
