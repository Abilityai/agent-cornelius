People tend to think about themselves as unique. We don’t always see ourselves as the best, but always as unique. We also think that everyone is very different, but they are actually more similar than expected. Since we think people are too different we don’t trust their emotions about the future. 

That's why [[Best way to estimate future feelings is to ask someone in this position, not imagine]]
