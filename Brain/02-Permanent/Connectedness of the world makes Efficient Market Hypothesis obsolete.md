[[EMH - Efficient Market Hypothesis]]  [[Wisdom of a crowd]]. 

[[Efficient Market Hypothesis is a form of Wisdom of a crowd]], but  [[Wisdom of a crowd only works without social information exchange]]. 
So the more information exchange there's in the world - the more possibility there's for [[Social Proof]] and [[influence]], which adds errors and [[Noise]] to individual judgments.
