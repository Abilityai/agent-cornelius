Very rarely [[Forecast]]s in the real world are validated rigorously. This is because there's no demand for it. Most of the customers of forecasts are not requesting [[Accuracy]] data on the forecasts. 

Thus those who make bad forecasts are not punished and keep doing their jobs like many political analysts who consistently wrongly predict elections.
