[[Explanation of bad things can makes us feel better]]

But in the same way, [[explanation]] of the Good things softens the positive impact of those things. 

Un-explained things seem rare and unusual, until they are explained. These things cause a lot stronger emotional response. One of the reasons for it is that we continue thinking about unexplained things.
