[[Leadership]] [[Business]] [[Entrepreneurship]] [[Productive Group]] [[psychology]] [[Management]]

1. Align the [[team]] towards the same clear goal. Make sure they all know what they are working towards. Over-communicate the goal.
2. Make sure they believe that you and their colleagues have the same shared goal, same agenda. This requires quite a lot of effort. People are doubtful.
3. If [[Conflict]] appears - remind the group that everyone is going towards the same goal and switch it to [[Constructive Conflict]]
