Flow is a mental state that is considered the most powerful state for high-performance people - athletes, businessmen etc. This is the state where people tend to experience a lot of joy, where brain chemistry works together, directed to achieve a progress. 

In the state of Flow you have the maximal attention to what is happening, this is a pure focus on here and now.
