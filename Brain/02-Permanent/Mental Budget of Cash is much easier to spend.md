[[Mental Budgeting - people have mental buckets that they spend from]]

Wealth is separated into different budgets in the mind. Cash (easiest to spend) → Current account → Savings account → Long term savings → Pension Fund.

If something is reclassified as Cash it becomes so much easier to spend. 

It could be one of the approaches for marketing to re-classify something as Cash.
