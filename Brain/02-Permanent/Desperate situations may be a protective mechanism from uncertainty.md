[[Existential risks in desperate situations take away decision freedom]]

Since desperate situations don't leave you any room for decision-making - you have to focus your attention on one big thing. People may [[Subconscious]]ly put themselves in desperate situations to not deal with [[Uncertainty]] and [[Decision Making]]. This is a psychological protection from the complexity of the world.
