When people run into the fact that contradicts their belief - they tend to change the [[Standard of Proof]] in order to have a reason to stay with the same belief. 

People use the [[Standard of Proof]] that confirms their story - softer one for the facts that prove our story, and harder ones for the ones we want to disprove. This is a form of [[Confirmation Bias]]
