When I walk around other stores, I see dozens of other options of the same kind of things, which results in:

1.  The head-to-head comparison makes me pay attention to criteria I did not care about before. This creates a multitude of new ‚Äúscales‚Äù, or [[Reference Frame]] in our minds. 3.  The last nail in this coffin is that the more options you have - the less satisfied you are with the choice you have made.
